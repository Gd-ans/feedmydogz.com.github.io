Pay only for what you buy

Online Shopping is trendy and convenient now these days, and shopping for Goods is more popular than ever, and there have never been so many ways to do it. So why not do it with a cause and make a purchase that combines the good feelings of pampering your pet while you help the homeless paws.

All products under our shopping cart are customized and cruel free which you and your pets will love; while you pamper your pet with the things they love you also get to help our helpless paws on the street. They look for your love too.

We at FeedMyDogz pledge to contribute 100% of the profits of all these products to the FeedMyDogz trust. We at FeedMyDogz are committed and love to take care of the strays at our grace for the necessities of life. We are motivated to provide them with healthy food and water in the most hygienic way possible. We urge you to join the cause by shopping for your pet, and your treatment of your loving pet will be a blessing to the less fortunate ones; we assure you we will convey your love and care to them.
